Club Pavilia - Hong Kong's first residential clubhouse embodied with distinctive super yacht features. The well-built and pioneering Club Pavilia was designed and inspired by the international renowned super yacht designer Philippe Briand. It is well-equipped with an indoor swimming pool, children splash pool, Jacuzzi, Sauna, multi-function room, gym room, billiard room and ball room etc.

Philippe Briand

Vitruvius Yachts Ltd
Company Director

"A yacht is to always please the eye and be the pride of her owner."


Philippe is an award-winning French Naval Architect designing sailing yachts, motor yachts and mega yachts.


He was born into a family where racing is a long-established tradition. Inspired by his father, an Olympian sailor, Philippe began his career in France as a dinghy sailor at the tender age of 9.
